SanAI Privacy Policy 🚗💻  

Welcome to SanAI! Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it. This policy explains how we collect, use, and store your information.

---

🛡️ Data Collected and Stored

1. Login Information  
- Login on SanAI is conducted via Discord.  
- We do not store confidential information, such as Discord account passwords.  
- Only the username provided by Discord is stored to identify the author of chats created on the platform.  

2. Server Logs  
- During your session, logs may be generated by the server for diagnostic and troubleshooting purposes.  
- These logs may include:  
  - Actions performed on the platform.  
  - Errors or issues encountered during usage.  

3. AI-Generated Messages  
- Messages generated by SanAI may be stored and analyzed to improve the quality of service and AI features.  

---

📦 How Do We Use Your Data?  
- To identify chat authors, facilitating organization and interaction.  
- To analyze logs for bug identification and technical issue resolution.  
- To continuously improve AI responses and functionality.  

---

🔒 Security and Storage  
- We use industry-standard practices to protect stored information from unauthorized access.  
- We do not share your data with third parties without your consent.  

---

📞 Contact Us  
If you have questions about this privacy policy or how your data is used, please contact us on GitHub: https://github.com/JuniorSchueller/SanAI
